Deliver and debug your emails reliably.

== Description ==

Better email by [Ignite Online](https://igniteonline.com.au) lets you deliver your emails reliably via AWS Ses or debug them quickly without sending spam to your clients on Mailtrap.
The plugin reads your website environment or the WP_DEBUG mode to understand where to send your emails.
This plugins requires an account on both [Mailtrap](https://mailtrap.io/) and [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/) to work correctly.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `ignite-better-email` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Ignite' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
